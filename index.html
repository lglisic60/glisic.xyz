Governance NodesFORTHCOMING

Technical specification, draft v1 · Presented at the Stanford Blockchain Governance Summit, 2026

A single immutable smart contract that stores governance logic as state. Governance systems are created by composing versioned, forkable Nodes — quorums, timelocks, vetoes, governors — rather than by deploying a custom contract for every use case.
